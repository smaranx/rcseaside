About Us



Rotaract Club of New Bombay Seaside

Rotaract Club of New Bombay Seaside was chartered on 26th July 2016 by its Partner Rotary, Rotary Club of New Bombay Seaside. Established with the noble aim of fostering young leaders and instilling a spirit of community service, this club has continued to thrive over the years. Currently in its 8th year, the new dynamic team of RCNBS brings with them a passion for service and a vision for the Rotaract Year 2023-24.
